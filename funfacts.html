Here are 3 birds from Ontario that I find interesting/beautiful/fascinating.

  1. Home Sparrow
  2. Parrot
  3. Columbidae

Home Sparrow

The House Sparrow is one of the most common birds in the world, found on every continent except Antarctica.

Parrot

Parrots are known for their ability to mimic human speech and other sounds. While not all parrots can talk, some species are particularly good at it, such as the African grey parrot and the Amazon parrot.

Columbidae

Columbidae is one of the most diverse bird families, with over 300 species found around the world. Doves and pigeons are closely related and are often difficult to distinguish from each other. In fact, the term "pigeon" is often used to describe larger species of doves.
